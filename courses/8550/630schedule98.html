 Comprehensive Final Exam - 12 noon.

Each student will make an oral presentation on the subject of their choice. The topic must pertain to some aspect of clay mineralogy (either a lab project or a library referenced presentation is possible). An abstract, outline, figures and list of references should be provided to each class participant.

Final grade is determined as follows: Midterm exam (20%), Final exam (30%), Labs (40%), Oral presentation (5%), Class project (5%).
